01 · The work

A small studio that scales like a collective.

Geografa runs lean by default and assembles the right team from a trusted network of engineers, designers, and developers — under one P&E manager so support and continuity stay high-touch.

Optimization

Slow tiles, heavy bundles, jittery interactions — we audit your stack and bring map performance back into spec. Vector tile pipelines, style refactors, render budgets.

Custom map products

From a single embed to a full application — Mapbox GL, MapLibre, Three.js, vector tiles, custom geocoding, geofencing, 3D models. Delivered as code your team can own.

Strategy & advisory

Choosing a basemap provider, scoping a migration, hiring your first geo engineer. Twenty years of "I've seen this before" — applied to the decisions that matter most.

03 · How we work

Geografa is committed to a customer- and client-centered approach — assembling just the right team for each project, then keeping the relationship close as it grows.

Most engagements start with a conversation, not a SOW. We listen first, then scope: what's already working, where the bottlenecks are, who on your team will own this after we hand it off.

From there, we tap a committed network of engineers, designers, and developers and assemble the smallest team that can ship the work well. Everything routes through one P&E manager so you get high-touch, consistent account support — no rotating contacts, no relearning your stack on every new ticket.

And we leave you the code, the docs, and a clear way to keep going without us.

— Rafa
